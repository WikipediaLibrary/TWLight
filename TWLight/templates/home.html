Benefits

The Wikipedia Library provides free access to research materials to improve your ability to contribute content to Wikimedia projects.

Through the Library Card you can apply for access to more than 60 leading publishers of reliable sources including over 100,000 unique journals that would otherwise be paywalled. Use just your Wikipedia login to sign up. Coming soon... direct access to resources using only your Wikipedia login!

If you think you could use access to one of our partner resources and are an active editor in any project supported by the Wikimedia Foundation, {% trans "please apply" %}.
